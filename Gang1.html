Laboratorio 3

Juan Diego Solorzano 18151



Progress

The year is 1922 and the stakes are high here in New York. Jerry Smith, your older brother, was just killed by an unknown enemy earlier today and beside him was note that read “Welcome to the big leagues”. Who knows if The Whyos, the Eastman Gang or any other New York gang was responsible for this, but we do know that something must be done. You enter the Gander, the Plaw’s safehouse, and you see every significant member of the group desperately waiting for orders. Now that your brother, who was leader of the Plaw gang, is gone, you are expected to fill his shoes and take the business to new heights. You take the stage and its time to give your first impression as new leader of the gang.
You decide to:

  • Leave the gang fearing you might die next
  • Wage war against The Whyos and Eastman Gang
